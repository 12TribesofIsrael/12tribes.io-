The so-called Central American Indians' from Guatemala to Panama: The Haven of Ships.

The habitation of Zebulun borders both the Atlantic and the Pacific Ocean, making it a haven for ships. Like many of the tribes in Central and South America and the Caribbean Islands, a majority of Zebulun have succumbed to the Roman Catholic belief. Zebulun according to the Bible would rejoice in his going out' When you examine the land of Zebulun. he is surrounded by his brethren of the tribes of Israel. Murder and Disease such as smallpox ravage from Guatemala all the way through Panama during the early 16th century under the hand of the Spaniards (American Holocaust pg. 80-82).
